About Us

Furaha Crest Academy is a learner-centered institution founded on the values of Faith, Unity, Responsibility, Academic Excellence, Holistic Growth, and Attitude of Care. We provide quality, holistic education in a safe and nurturing environment where every child is valued, supported, and inspired to achieve their full potential through a Competency-Based Education (CBE) approach. We focus on holistic talent development, helping each learner discover and grow their unique abilities.
