Academic Programs (Competency-Based Education – CBE)

At Furaha Crest Academy, learning is guided by the CBE curriculum, focusing on skills, values, creativity, and practical learning.

Playgroup & Pre-Primary (PP1 & PP2)

  • Play-based learning for early development.
  • Early literacy and numeracy.
  • Social skills, creativity and values.

Lower Primary (Grades 1–3)

  • Literacy and numeracy mastery.
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving.
  • Creativity, digital skills, and responsibility.

Upper Primary (Grades 4–6)

  • Application of knowledge and skills.
  • Leadership, teamwork and innovation.
  • Holistic talent development based on each learner’s potential.
  • Life skills and co-curricular engagement.

Assessment

  • Continuous assessment and projects.
  • Practical activities and learner portfolios.
